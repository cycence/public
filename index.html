Cycence CY-GVR0434CV-R 32 Inch 4 Burner Professional Built-In Gas Grill + Infrared Rear Burner, Stainless Steel with Rotisseries Kit

DESCRIPTION

Powerful 4-Burner Gas Grill with Infrared Rear-Burner for Rotisserie

This Cycence processional four (4) 12,600 BTU tube burners and one (1) 15,300 BTU rear infrared burner, delivering exceptional grilling and searing results. The rear infrared rotisseries burner allows you to rotate meats, adding versatility to your outdoor cooking. This grill includes an Even-Heat Cooking System that distributes uniform heat throughout the firebox. Angled flame tamers deflect heat, prevent flare-ups and transform drippings into flavour vapours that infuse foods with a distinctive smoky taste. Electric ignition allows it to fire up instantly and the built-in thermometer enables precise temperature regulation. And if that wasn't enough, this grill also equipped with rotisseries kit and low RPM motor.

Outdoor Kitchen Complement

It boasts 695 total square inches of cooking space to cook a meal for a family. The built-in thermometer helps you monitor the temperature easily and accurately.

Halogen Lights

Liquid Propane or Natural Gas
